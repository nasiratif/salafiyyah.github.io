Brief introductory to Salafīyyah.net

Assalāmu ‘Alaikum wa rahmatullāhi wa barakātuh.

By the grace and permission of Allāh, He (سبحانه و تعالى) has allowed me to create this website. Salafiyyah.net is a simple website that aims to give authentic statements of the Salaf (may Allāh be pleased with them all) in a way that is easy to navigate.

I hope you greatly benefit from this site and if you do, please make sure to share it to others so that others may benefit as well.

IMPORTANT: Some narrations posted on the site may have some inconsistencies, such narrations will be marked with a clickable question mark (?) at the end of the title which will bring you to a footnote explaining such inconsistency.
